Background Demo Data Submission

What is Changing?

We are now leveraging user information to create people (aka lead) records within our MarketoLive instance.

Why are we making this change?

In order to meet the folowing demo data requirements:

  • Real, pronounceable names
  • Diverse set of names from around the world
  • Organic growth of people flowing through the campaigns and automations
  • Organic decay of people's activities

In order to solve the following demo challenges:

  • Realistic distribution of times/days that pages are being visited and forms are being filled out
  • Realistic order of page visits (e.g. first Lead Capture then Preference Center)
  • Realisitcs rate of page visits (e.g. not simply once every day per person)
  • Realistic form conversion rates

What information is being leveraged?

  • First Name   [Brian]
  • Last Name   [Fisher]
  • User ID          [bfisher]

Who is the going to effect?

All INTERNAL users of the MarketoLive extension (this excludes partners).

Where is this information shared??

ONLY with our MarketoLive instance people (aka lead) database.

When is this change taking effect?

Immediately
